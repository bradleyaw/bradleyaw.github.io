About Me

I'm a competitor; someone who strives to be the best in whatever I am involved in both professionally and in my personal life. I take pride in providing solutions to problems, both cookie cutter and out of the box. I'm known for keeping my cool and helping my team or clients through tough situations.

When I'm not working, you're likely to find me in the sand, playing volleyball, or traveling around North Carolina.

After graduating NC State with a Communication Media degree, I worked for a small marketing team at an international sales training company and developed a wide breadth of digital skills including HTML/CSS coding, working in Ektron CMS, Google Adwords & Analytics campaign management, video production, and Salesforce/Pardot administration.

My most recent endeavour was into HOA management, where I guided several board of directors to make informed decisions best for their communities. Here I learned the true meaning of multitasking and meeting deadlines under pressure.
